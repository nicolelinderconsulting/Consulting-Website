About

A trusted partner in meaningful work.

A strategic CEO and brand architect with 25+ years leading high-growth organizations, Nicole has deep experience shaping corporate narrative, driving investor and donor response, crafting media exposure, and strengthening brand equity. She now applies that executive-level insight to support other leaders and organizations in enhancing strategic planning and positioning, communications, public relations, brand management, revenue generation, board governance, and leadership development. As an experienced non-profit fundraiser, Nicole has developed deep trust with donors to help them reach their philanthropic goals and interests.

"I firmly believe leadership is not done in a vacuum — nobody gets to where they are without helping hands, wonderful mentors, and people willing to tell you hard truths."
Experience
CEO, Marjaree Mason Center (2016–2026) — Scaled programs, increased impact and brand awareness, led crisis management during COVID, doubled operating budget, grew net assets by over 600%, designed, built and raised funds to produce a debt-free, trauma-informed facility.
Senior Development Officer, Valley Children's Healthcare Foundation (2013–2016)

Services

Expertise tailored to your mission.

Each engagement is custom-designed to address your organization's unique needs, culture, and long-term goals. No cookie-cutter solutions - only thoughtful, clear, and mission-aligned guidance.

01

Strategic Planning & Positioning

Facilitating long-range planning that aligns vision, mission, and measurable outcomes for sustainable organizational health and community impact.

02

Communications, Public Relations & Brand Management

Shaping corporate narrative, managing media exposure, and strengthening brand equity across donor, government, and public platforms. Directing crisis communication, agency rebranding, and stewarding internal and external narratives to align mission with public perception.

03

Keynote & Motivational Speaking

Delivering strong public speaking and presentations since a young age, Nicole is skilled as a media spokesperson, panelist and keynote and motivational speaker from subjects ranging from women's issues to leadership, fundraising to faith.

04

Fundraising & Donor Strategy

Leadership Development & Transition

Donor Advisor, Community Reinvestment & Impact
